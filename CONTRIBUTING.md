# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project! Here are some guidelines to keep in mind:

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repository**: Click the "Fork" button at the top right of the repository page.
2. **Clone your fork**: Use `git clone <your-fork-url>` to clone your repository locally.
3. **Create a branch**: Make a new branch for your changes with `git checkout -b your-branch-name`.
4. **Make your changes**: Make your changes in the codebase.
5. **Commit your changes**: Use `git commit -m "Brief description of changes"` to commit your changes.
6. **Push to your fork**: Use `git push origin your-branch-name` to push your changes.
7. **Create a Pull Request**: On GitHub, navigate to your fork and click on "New Pull Request".

## Code of Conduct
Please be respectful and constructive in your comments and interactions. We expect everyone to follow our [Code of Conduct](link-to-code-of-conduct).
